Some tips for using this interactive Game Of Thrones map and som general information:

  • This is a student project for the course Webbutveckling - Klientsidan at Stockholms University.
  • This is an interactive map, hover your mouse over the markers on the map or click them to reveal more information.
  • Zoom in using your mousewheel or left click the map twice.
  • Zoom out using your mousewheel or right click the map twice.
  • The map currently only supports the TV-version of Game Of Thrones.
  • To minimize the settings-window to the right, press the hide button.
  • Warning, this page may contain spoilers.
  • This page is mobile friendly.
  • Intellectual property of A Song of Ice and Fire, this map, all characters and all locations belong to George R.R. Martin.
